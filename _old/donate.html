Travelling is not a hobby for everyone

Not all journeys are as nice as Berlin-Lisbon, not everyone can cross borders so easily. Journeys are not always wanted, they are not always a hobby. However, we have in Berlin every weeks lots of refugees coming from a long journey. "Refugees or not Refugees" is no longer the question. The biggest issue we face now is how to integrate them.

It is exactly what the association "Museum of Islamic Art" is working on. They help refugees to become museum guides. I believe it's a great way to build a link between our cultures! By donating you could help them to carry out this project! You can make a donation through betterplace.org or directy on their website.
