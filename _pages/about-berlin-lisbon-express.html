Content

cycle-man.com was first designed to find hosts during my bike tour from Berlin to Lisbon.
In a second time, its content has been extended to help people planning their routes, providing with with tracks, maps, which have been recorded on the way. The website is edited, designed by me. Any questions or suggestions please drop me a message on instagram.
