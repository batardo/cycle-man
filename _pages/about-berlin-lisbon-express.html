About this blog

Creation

cycle-man.com was designed in 2016 to find hosts during my bike tour from Berlin to Lisbon.
Then, its content has been extended to help people planning their routes, providing with with tracks, maps, which have been recorded on the way. The website is edited, designed by myself. Any questions or suggestions please drop me a message on instagram.

Privacy

Further to your approval and in order to measure my audience, understand where my visitors come from and improve the usability of this website, Google Analytics cookies are being used. For more information about the data collected by Google Analytics, better check Google's support page.
