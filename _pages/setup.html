Pisa, December 2014

Batavus Course

Perfect for the road, good-old racing bike from the late 80's, steel frame, weight only 10 kilograms. It has been my bike for 2 years, and after 2.000 kilometers together, I can tell we make a great couple! It will be my bike for this trip to Lisbon. Only problem: as soons as you leave the road and go through dirt road, it's over.

Tubus Fly Rack

Touring Rack

As a good race bike, the batavous has no rack. I had to buy one (tubus fly) extremely light (not even 500 gramms) on which you can load up to 20 kilos. I definitely needed help to assembly it on my Ferrari!

In the Alps, near Genova, December 2014

Pannier bags

The Vaude ones I have (two at side, one on top) are linked to one each other, so it provides more stability. Its enough space there for some T-shirts and one air pump; you don't want to carry more! Be careful: they are not water-proof!
